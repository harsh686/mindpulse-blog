## Why "MindPulse"?

✅ **Short & Memorable**: 9 letters, easy to type  
✅ **SEO-Friendly**: Clear, searchable term  
✅ **Brandable**: Unique, not generic  
✅ **Meaning**: Captures the "pulse" of what's on people's minds  
✅ **Professional**: Works for serious content  
✅ **Emotional**: Connects to feelings and thoughts
